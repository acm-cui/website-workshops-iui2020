Submissions

Submissions should be sent to:

cui2020-submissions@cs.toronto.edu

Position papers should be 4 to 6 pages long, in the 2-column landscape SIGCHI extended abstract format and include a brief statement justifying the fit with the workshop's topic. Summaries of previous research are welcome if they contribute to the workshop's multidisciplinary goals. Submissions will be reviewed according to:

  • Fit with the workshop topic

  • Potential to contribute to the workshop goals

  • A demonstrated track of research in the workshop area (HCI or speech/multimodal/dialogue/conversation processing, with an interest in both areas).

Important Dates:

  • December 18th, 2019: Submission of position papers

  • January 10th, 2019: Notification of acceptance

  • January 24th, 2019: Camera-ready submissions
